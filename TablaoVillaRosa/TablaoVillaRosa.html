Tablao Villa Rosa

Villa Rosa is more than a place to enjoy a good flamenco show in Madrid. A Temple that carries the spread of flamenco culture and art by flag and that it shares with people coming from all parts of the world. A scene that magnifies and spreads the Madrid nights from Monday to Sunday with great dancers, guitarists and singers of the Flemish national scene, giving rise to a unique Andalusian atmosphere in the capital.

In Villa Rosa we can find a varied gastronomic proposal inspired by the most representative dishes of Spanish culture and, especially, in the delights of Andalusia. Undoubtedly, Villa Rosa is more than a flamenco tablao, an enclave where the magic of flamenco is kept alive with its shows, its gastronomy, its artists and all the people who make it possible that this burning flame is lit every day, that feeling What is flamenco.
