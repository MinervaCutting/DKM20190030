Hotel Emperador

Located in the heart of Madrid, Emperador is set next to Madrid’s Gran Vía. This elegant hotel offers a seasonal rooftop swimming pool and terrace with fantastic city views..

The elegant and stylish rooms offer accommodation with free WiFi, flat-screen TV and private bathroom with marble floors

Emperador is just 10 minutes’ walk from the Royal Palace and Puerta del Sol, Madrid’s main square. Santo Domingo, Plaza de España and Callao Metro Stations are within a 3-minute walk, connecting you with the famous Art Triangle in just 15 minutes..

Open from May to September, the Emperador offers a seasonal swimming pool and solarium with sun loungers. The cocktail-bar located at the terrace boasts with views of the Royal Palace, Almudena Cathedral and Gran Vía Avenue. There is also a fitness centre, sauna and massage service available upon request.
